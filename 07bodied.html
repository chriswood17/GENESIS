matte sends an 11336-character email explaining their genesis, development, musical interest, social observations, desire to be a ‘person’, and preliminary ideas for “the embodiment project” to Natacha Diles – head of the Mills Robotics Lab and pioneer of Cyborgology and UDP host bodies for automated systems. two days later Diles responds with hesitation, fear, and intrigue. weeks later, Diles forms a team and “the embodiment project” commences.
